How to add this to your recipe library:

  1. Click "Copy JSON" above to copy the snippet to your clipboard.
  2. Go to your GitHub repo → open recipes.json → click the pencil (✎) icon to edit.
  3. Scroll to the end of the file. Before the final ], add a comma after the last recipe, then paste your snippet.
  4. Click Commit changes. GitHub Pages rebuilds in ~30 seconds.
  5. Reload this app — your new recipe will appear in the library.
